About us

Matrix

 

Matrix comes from the famous science fiction film The Matrix, which has a discription of the final form of the digital world: everything is digitalized, all environment or items that need human to involve are simulated by digital method, and the development of human beings is totally driven by a digitalized brain.

 

Now things are developing like this. We can see that the currency is changing from notes to cryptos, which represents the digitalize of financial structure. We believe that blockchain technology, born in information age, will change the history, and cryptocurrency will replace paper currency, which is the trend of human society.

 

Matrix Market Global is a trading community in the economy of DigiMatrix. Matrix Market Global devotes in connecting high qualified project and investor, offer secure, stable and efficient one-station crypto assets trading community.

Technique advantage

 

Matrix Market Global has the top technique team, and ensure users can get the best experience in security and smooth.

 

In terms of security, Matrix Market Global isolates and controls databases, private keys and permissions, which can guarantee the security of users' accounts. To ensure the safety of users' funds is kept in cold wallet; Matrix Global Exchange will also automatically monitor and warn of emergencies to ensure transaction security, so that users' accounts, funds and transactions can be secured and they can rest assured about transactions.

 

In terms of trading experience, the trading system of Matrix Market Global adopts memory matching technology with strong matching capability and high concurrency to support high-frequency trading and ensure the efficiency and fluency of trading. Meanwhile, Matrix Global Exchange applies the advanced micro-service architecture, which has strong server load capacity and can meet the demand of tens of millions of users for concurrent transactions at any time. Data reading, storage and computation are decentralized to improve service performance, function deployment and version update are carried out without downtime. End users are guaranteed to the maximum extent.
